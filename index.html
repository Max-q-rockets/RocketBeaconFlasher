Your assigned amateur radio callsign. A–Z, 0–9, optional /suffix.

minutes The callsign has its own schedule, separate from the bursts, and is sent at the first gap after the interval elapses (never mid-burst). In the US, FCC §97.119 requires ID at least every 10 minutes, so set this slightly under 10 if you want margin. The ID transmits at max power, in FM for FM/Both modes.

Firmware source & build notes (for the maintainer)

The base binary is built once from the rocketbeacon source with a patchable config block (config version 4), then committed to firmware/rocketbeacon.bin in this repository. This tool fetches it, patches your settings in, and recomputes the checksum — visitors never compile or upload anything.

Four files make up the firmware change: beacon_config.h, beacon_config.c, a linker-script section, and the edits to main.c. The full beacon_config.h, beacon_config.c, and main.c are provided alongside this tool; the config struct and linker section are summarised below.
